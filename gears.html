Gears

Have you ever wondered what is the order of your gear ratios on your bike? When you want to downshift from 2-6, but not quite down to 2-5, if 3-4 would do the trick? Is your bike or your friend's bike faster in the highest gear, accounting for tire size too? One way is to calculate the development, that is, the distance you travel during one full revolution of the crankset, for all gears. This simple development plotter will do the job for you.
